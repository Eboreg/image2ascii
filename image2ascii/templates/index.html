Image2ASCII

Outputs in glorious ANSI colour.
Crops empty areas at all edges.
Fills characters that would have been empty, and vice versa.
Inverts the colours of the image before processing.
Makes black output characters white and vice versa; does not affect any other colours.
If checked, all sections of the image (except for any transparent ones) will be filled. Does not make any sense unless "colour" is also checked.
